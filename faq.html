Frequently Asked Questions

Why are you doing this?

Our only motivation for doing this is to help missionaries save time and money and to give as many people as possible the opportunity to be blessed by hosting a missionary.

We believe the Bible makes it clear that God rewards those who take even the smallest part in missions (Matthew 10:42) and that hosting and entertaining missionaries brings helps the gospel and rewwards the hosts (Hebrews 13:2).

How will Mission Hosts make money?

It won't. This is not and will never be a paid app and it isn't our intention to make any money with it. God has blessed us beyond measure in our other work. Any and all donations will be used 100% to further this work.

Why are you raising money?

We could do this in our spare time and raise no money for it, but it would take A LOT longer to launch. Raising a small ammount of money allows one of our developers to focus on it for a few months (losing regular employment) and keep his lights on.

Can hosts charge missionaries?

Missionhosts is intended to be a ministry, not a business opportunity for hosts. Hosts can suggest a donation for cleaning/utilities ect. but the app will not fascilitate any money exchanges.

Will this just be prophet's chambers?

No. We want this to be a resource for anyone willing to host missionaries. Including church prophet's chambers, individuals with a spare apartment, available RV hookups and even families willing to have missionaries in their home for a few days.

How will you vet missionaries/hosts?

Every missionary and host will have to fill out a detailed profile. We will review each profile and call each missionary and host. Hosts will be able to review the missionaries profile before accepting a stay request and missionaries will be able to see photos and comments of the hosts before requesting a stay.

How will you keep people's contact info private?

All communication between the hosts and missionaries will happen through the app to sheild hosts personal contact details.
