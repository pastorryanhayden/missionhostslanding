Missionaries spend far too much time looking for housing.

We are building a simple app to put missionaries on deputation/furlough in contact with people and churches who will host them.

Missionaries on deputation need a place to sleep between meetings.

A typical independent baptist missionary will spend years on the road raising support. They typically have a place to stay on Saturday and Sunday, but spend hours every week and thousands of dollars on a place to stay during the week. We want to fix that.

Help Travelling Missionaries

This app will be a great help to travelling missionaries, enabling them to easily find places to stay between meetings and saving them thousands of dollars in hotel costs.

  • ✓ Find willing hosts
  • ✓ Cut out the phone tag
  • ✓ Find the ammenities they need (wifi, kids room, laundry, etc)

Save Hosts Time

This app will help missionary hosts by cutting down on the ammount of back and forth conversations.

  • ✓ Check missionary profile
